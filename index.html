Research

I'm currently working as postdoctoral associate in Vivek Jayaraman's lab at Janelia. I am interested in understanding how animals navigate through space. Currently I invstigate how visually guided navigation in walking fruit flies can change based on past experiences. Previous projects involved different aspects of navigation in adult and larval fruit flies and crickets.

(Data) Visualisation

One of my favorite parts of quantitatively studying behavior is to come up with ways to visualise the measured variables. In my experience, finding the "right" way of looking at the data often leads to better understanding of what is going on.
